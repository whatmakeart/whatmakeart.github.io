## Overview

Studio work sewing inflatable exercise and assembling into a collaborative inflatable. Students present their ideation sketches and discuss plans on how to refine their ideas in order to be sewn as a cold air inflatable.

## Learning Objectives

- Sew together a modular piece of a larger inflatable
- Sew a tube to an interior hole
- Sew interior baffles into the exercise inflatable to help keep its shape

## Key Terms

- Modular
- Interior Baffle
- Cold Air Inflatable
