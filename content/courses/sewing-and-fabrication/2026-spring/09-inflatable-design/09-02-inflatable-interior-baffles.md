---
title: 09.02 Inflatable Interior Baffles
date: 2026-03-30T09:00:00-04:00
lastmod: 2026-04-04T07:46:04-04:00
---

Inflatables naturally become round and fill the available space permitted by the exterior membrane. Interior baffles are needed to keep the form from expanding outward and to create any concave curves or forms.
