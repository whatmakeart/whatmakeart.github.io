## Assignment Deliverables

1. Sewn Upper Pants Block (without zipper fly)

## Assignment Overview

Using your pants pattern pieces, cut out truncated front and back pants pieces from scrap fabric. Sew the pieces together following the [How to Sew Pants](../../../../sewing/how-to-sew-pants.md) instructions, skipping the zipper fly. Test if the sizing of the sewn fabric matches the original garment that you copied the pattern from. Now is the time to adjust any fit issues on your pattern pieces.
