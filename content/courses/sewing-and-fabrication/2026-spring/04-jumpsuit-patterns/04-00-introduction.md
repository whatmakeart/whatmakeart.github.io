## Overview

Students combine copied pattern blocks into jumpsuit patterns. Students learn garment construction techniques via assembly exercises. Inserting a zipper into a fly allows students to incorporate a zipper into their finished jumpsuits if desired. Flat construction techniques are used for efficiency and accuracy. Understitching is used to keep facings pressed over without revealing the seam.

## Learning Objectives

- cut out pieces to assemble a zipper fly pants opening
- sew a zipper into the front of a pants

- understiching
