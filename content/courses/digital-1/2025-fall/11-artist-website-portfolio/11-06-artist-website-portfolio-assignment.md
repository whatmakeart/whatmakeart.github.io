## Assignment Deliverables

1. Screenshot of portfolio website
   - Label files YYYY-MM-DD Lastname Firstname portfolio website screenshot (`.jpg`, `.png`)
2. URl(s) of portfolio website(s)
   - Label files YYYY-MM-DD Lastname Firstname portfolio website url (`.docx`, `.pdf`)

## Assignment Overview

An online presence can be cruicial for a creative professional's career and for sharing their work. While it is [not necessary to have a website to be an artist](./11-04-artist-website-portfolio.md), it is a good idea to have an online portfolio unless you have a specific reason to not have one.

### Prepare Images

1. Gather images of your work.
2. Crop, adjust, and optimize your images for the web.
3. Label images with your first and lastname and a brief description. Use dashes instead of spaces.

### Natural Language Programming Process

1. Locate your main repository from GitHub. This is the one with your `username.github.io`.
2. Clone the repository to your computer or make sure it is on your computer.
3. Put the images folder in your repository.
4. Use GitHub Copilot to design a web gallery.
5. Put the images in a folder in your repository.
6. Make changes as needed.
7. Commit and Publish your changes.
8. Take a screenshot of your website.

### Cargo Process

1. Create a [cargo.site](https://cargo.site/) account
2. Use the promo code provided by your instructor
3. Use a template and add your content.
4. Take a screenshot of your website.
