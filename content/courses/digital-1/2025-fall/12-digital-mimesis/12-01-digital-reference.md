Artists absorb and assimilate influences constantly. Part of the creative workflow included the collection of references and inspiration. Often this can be physical artifacts such as fabric samples, paint swatches, magazine clippings and any other of the numerous objects from the world.

Digital tools can streamline the collection of references but potentially suffer from the inability to quickly see and browse long buried digital files
