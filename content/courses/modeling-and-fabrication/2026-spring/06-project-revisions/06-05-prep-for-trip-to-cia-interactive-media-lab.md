- Lunch or money to purchase lunch _Note: Currently no nearby businesses accept Case Cash_
- Laptop + charger
- Jacket
- Physical Object to 3D Scan _Something smallish and easy to transport_

## CIA Interactive Media Lab

### Address

Midtown Collaboration Center
1974 E. 66th Street
Cleveland, OH 44103

### Public Transportation from CIA

RTA Day Passes are available at the Help Desk.

The Healthline can take you to the Midtown Collaboration Center. The closest station at CIA is Euclid Ave & E. 115th St. Station. Take the transit for 11 stops until the Euclid Ave & E. 66th St. Station. After getting off, walk to the front entrance on E. 66th St. Euclid Ave. doors are MCC access card only.

CIA front desk is located up the yellow stairs, to the left.
