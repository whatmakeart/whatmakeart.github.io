[Fab](https://www.fab.com/) is Epic Games online marketplace that has lots of 3D assets that can be downloaded and used to create 3D environments. Be careful and aware that often models may be copyrighted even if it says CC Creative Commons.

[BlenderKit](https://www.blenderkit.com/) is an integrated addon for Blender that allows 3D assets to download directly into [Blender](../../../../3d-modeling/blender/blender.md).

[Polyhaven](https://polyhaven.com/) is a great source for reliably Creative Commons and Royalty Free 3D models, textures, and HDRIs.
