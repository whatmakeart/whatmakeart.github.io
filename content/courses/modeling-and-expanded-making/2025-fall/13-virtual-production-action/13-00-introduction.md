## Overview

Students work together to capture virtual production content for their projects. Retargting of motion capture data to character meshes with armatures is demonstrated.

## Learning Objectives

- Plan out different shots in an Unreal environment for virtual production
- Use blueprints in Unreal to save camera shot locations
- Expose variables to disguise with the Event Tick to select previously saved shots
