Watch the video about the [Autodesk Fusion user interface](https://youtu.be/YjaxBbTY3kc). It explains [Fusion](fusion-360.md) works with project files. It also shows how to navigate in 3D space with your three button mouse. Fusionx is a [parametric modeling](../parametric-modeling.md) program and therefore has a design history timeline with all of the features of your 3D model. This video gives you the information that you need to get started with the basics in Fusion. The [Fusion FAQ](fusion-360-faq.md) has answers to common Fusion 360 modeling questions.
