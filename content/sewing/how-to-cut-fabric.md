### Fabric Cutting Tips

- Keep the bottom of the scissors pressed against the table for a more accurate cut.
- Don't fully close the scissors on a long length of cutting to prevent little nicks and notches.
- Use fabric weights or pins to keep the fabric from shifting or moving.
