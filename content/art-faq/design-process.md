The design process serves as the essential workflow and approach for creators and makers. Attempting to jump straight to a finished idea or product is a seductive trap, usually leading to wasted time, materials and frustration. By embracing an iterative cycle, you allow the project to evolve through trial and error, identifying flaws while theyâ€™re still just concepts rather than structural failures. The design process almost always begins with the low stakes thinking and sketching, where ideas can be tested, trashed, and refined in a space where the only cost of a mistake is a bit of paper and some time.
