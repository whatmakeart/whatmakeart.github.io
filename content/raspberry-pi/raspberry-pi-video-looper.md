### 4. Set Up Pi with Screen

1. Attach the Pi to the video monitor with an HDMI cable.
2. Put the video files on a USB drive and put the drive in the Pi.
3. Insert the video looper disk image SD card into the Pi SD card slot.
4. Power everything on and enjoy the automatically looping videos.
